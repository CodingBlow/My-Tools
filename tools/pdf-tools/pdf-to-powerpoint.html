Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the PDF to PowerPoint conversion?
Our converter maintains high accuracy for text, images, and basic layouts. Complex elements like animations or interactive features in the original PDF may not transfer perfectly. For best results, use PDFs with clear layouts and standard fonts.
Is my data secure when using this converter?
Yes, your data is completely secure. Our PDF to PowerPoint converter processes files locally in your browser, so your documents never leave your device. We don't store, share, or upload your files to any server.
What's the maximum file size I can convert?
Our tool supports PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, we recommend splitting your PDF into smaller documents before conversion for optimal performance and results.
Can I convert password-protected PDFs?
Yes, you can convert password-protected PDFs by entering the password when prompted during the upload process. The conversion will proceed normally after successful authentication.
How do I edit the PowerPoint after conversion?
After downloading the converted PPTX file, you can open and edit it using Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Slides, LibreOffice Impress, or any other presentation software that supports the PPTX format. All elements should be editable as in a normal presentation.

Tips for Better Conversions

Use high-quality PDFs for better results
Higher quality PDFs with clear text and images will convert more accurately. If possible, use PDFs created directly from digital sources rather than scanned documents.
Enable OCR for scanned documents
If your PDF contains scanned text or images with text, enable the OCR (Optical Character Recognition) feature to extract editable text. This will make your PowerPoint slides much more useful for editing.
Choose the right layout for your content
Select a slide layout that matches your intended use. Widescreen (16:9) is best for modern displays and projectors, while 4:3 may work better for older equipment. A4 or Letter layouts are ideal if you plan to print your slides.
Review and edit after conversion
Always review your converted presentation and make any necessary adjustments. You may need to realign some elements, adjust text formatting, or resize images for the best appearance.
