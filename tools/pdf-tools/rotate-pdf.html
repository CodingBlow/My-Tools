Why Rotate PDF Pages Online?

Our PDF rotation tool provides a simple solution for correcting improperly oriented pages in your documents. Whether you scanned documents upside down, received incorrectly oriented files, or need to adjust page layout for presentation, our tool makes it easy to fix these issues directly in your browser.

Unlike desktop software that requires installation, our online PDF rotator works on any device with a web browser. All processing happens locally on your computer, ensuring your sensitive documents never leave your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to rotate PDFs with this tool?
Yes! Our tool processes your PDF entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.
Can I rotate multiple pages at once?
Currently, you can rotate pages individually. For batch rotation, you would need to rotate each page separately before downloading the modified PDF.
What's the maximum file size I can rotate?
There are no file size limits since processing happens in your browser. However, very large files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.
Does this work on mobile devices?
Yes! Our PDF rotation tool is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets as well as desktop computers.
Will rotating affect the quality of my PDF?
No, rotating pages is a lossless operation that maintains the original quality of your document. Text remains selectable and searchable.
